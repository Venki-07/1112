Why donate blood

Save lives

Your donation supports surgeries, trauma care, cancer treatment, and rare conditions.

Community impact

Local blood banks rely on regular donors to meet daily demands and crisis surges.

Free health check

Every donation includes a quick screening that can flag potential health issues early.

Basic eligibility

Age

Typically 18–65 years. Check local guidelines for specifics.

Weight

Usually minimum 50 kg for whole blood donation.

Health

In good general health, no active infections, and adequate hemoglobin.

Interval

At least 3 months between whole blood donations.

Always verify with your local blood bank. Eligibility criteria may vary by country and organization.
